# Lunar Hack 2.0 - Official Requirements Summary

## Core Challenge
Build an AI platform that makes any SME (PME) "smart" by combining:
- Data analytics
- Marketing automation
- Conversational intelligence
- Content generation

## Mandatory Scope
- Cover at least 2 of the 3 pillars.
- Depth and real utility are more important than feature count.

## Pillars
1. Smart Analytics Dashboard
- Real-time KPIs
- Auto AI insights
- Actionable recommendations

2. Sales Intelligence Agent
- Conversational lead engagement
- Need qualification
- Personalized commercial recommendations

3. Marketing AI Assistant
- Generate communication ideas
- Marketing content and visuals
- Text adapted to product, audience, campaign

## Example Expectations (Guidance, Not Constraints)
- Dashboard: KPIs + trend insight + actionable recommendation
- Sales agent: asks qualification questions, detects intent, returns tailored pitch
- Marketing assistant: generates visual idea, caption, 3 tones, hashtags, best posting time

## Deliverables (Important)
- GitHub repository with documentation (README and comments)
- Demo video (max 2 minutes)
- User manual (max 10 pages)
- Functional prototype with at least 2 pillars
- Concrete use case in a real sector
- Live demonstration
- Pitch deck (5 min presentation + 5 min Q&A)

## Strategic Guidance from Brief
- Choose a specific target sector.
- Build 2 pillars deeply rather than 3 shallowly.
- Keep solution coherent, useful, and demo-ready.
- Suggested tools are non-restrictive (LLMs, agents, automation, databases, dashboards, n8n, APIs, web/mobile apps).

## Architecture Implication for Your Team
Given your current focus, strongest path is:
- Pillar 03 (Marketing AI Assistant) done deeply by your stream
- Merge with either Pillar 01 or Pillar 02 from teammates
- Ensure final integrated prototype clearly demonstrates at least 2 pillars end-to-end in one user story
