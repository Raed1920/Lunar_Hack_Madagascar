# Policies and Legal Notes

## Refund Policy
- Services are billed monthly in advance.
- Cancellation requires 14-day notice before next billing cycle.
- One-time setup fees are non-refundable once implementation starts.

## Data Privacy
- Customer data is processed under least-privilege access.
- No customer PII is shared with third parties outside approved integrations.
- Data retention defaults to 12 months unless client requests earlier deletion.

## Compliance Scope
- Guidance aligns with common GDPR-style and consumer-protection practices.
- Legal guidance is informational and not a substitute for licensed legal advice.

## Communication Policy
- Standard support SLAs: first response within 1 business day.
- Strategic reviews are delivered bi-weekly for active clients.
