# Marketing Guides

## Ecommerce Growth Blueprint
1. Diagnose funnel drop-off points.
2. Prioritize high-intent traffic acquisition.
3. Build segmented retention automations.
4. Iterate on product-page and checkout UX.
5. Scale budgets only when CAC and ROAS thresholds are met.

## Lead Qualification Framework
- Business type
- Monthly budget range
- Top 1-2 goals
- Decision timeline
- Buying intent signals

## Recommended Channel Mix by Goal
- Increase immediate sales: paid social + search + retargeting
- Improve retention: email automation + loyalty flows
- Raise average order value: upsell journeys + bundles + post-purchase offers
