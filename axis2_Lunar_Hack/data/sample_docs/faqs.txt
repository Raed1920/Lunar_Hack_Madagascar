# FAQ

## How fast can campaigns launch?
Campaigns can launch in 7-14 days after discovery and asset approval.

## Do you support multilingual campaigns?
Yes. We support English, French, and Arabic campaign workflows.

## Can the team work with small budgets?
Yes. Starter campaigns can run with USD 1,000-3,000 ad budgets, but scale potential is limited.

## What KPIs are tracked?
Core KPIs include conversion rate, CAC, ROAS, average order value, and repeat purchase rate.

## Is there a minimum contract?
The recommended minimum engagement is 3 months to gather enough optimization data.
